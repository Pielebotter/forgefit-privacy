Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2024

Introduction

Forge: Fit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Forge: Fit collects the following information to provide our fitness tracking service:

  • Name: Used for personalization within the app
  • Body Metrics: Weight and height for tracking progress and calculating BMI
  • Fitness Data: Workout history, exercises, sets, reps, and weights you log
  • Preferences: Your fitness goals, experience level, and unit preferences

How Your Data is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not:

  • Transmit your data to external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Sell your personal information

Your data remains on your device and is never uploaded to any cloud service or server.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and screen lock.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the application from your device. This will permanently remove all data associated with the app.

In-App Purchases

Forge: Fit offers optional premium subscriptions. All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Forge: Fit is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
